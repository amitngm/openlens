## Verification Steps Before Choosing New Name

### 1. **Trademark Search**
- Search USPTO (United States Patent and Trademark Office)
- Search EUIPO (European Union Intellectual Property Office)
- Search WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organization)
- Use services like: Justia, Trademarkia, USPTO.gov

### 2. **Company Name Search**
- Search business registries in your target markets
- Check Secretary of State databases (US)
- Check Companies House (UK)
- Check other relevant jurisdictions

### 3. **Domain Availability**
- Check domain registrars (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
- Check for .com, .io, .dev, .app domains
- Check social media handles (@flowstack, etc.)

### 4. **Legal Consultation**
- Consult with trademark attorney
- Get professional opinion on name availability
- Understand risks and mitigation strategies
