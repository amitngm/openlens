# Branding Strategy: Product Name Selection

## Selected Name: **FlowLens** ✅

### Why FlowLens?

1. **Memorable & Short** - Easy to remember, pronounce, and type
2. **Emphasizes Unique Feature** - "Lens" suggests visibility/observability (key differentiator)
3. **Professional** - Sounds enterprise-ready, modern
4. **Brandable** - Easy to create logo, tagline, marketing materials
5. **SEO-Friendly** - "Flow" + "Lens" are common search terms
6. **Differentiates** - Not generic, emphasizes observability/visibility
7. **Available** - No trademark conflicts found

### Tagline:
- **"FlowLens: See Every Flow"** ⭐ (Primary tagline)
- "FlowLens: Complete Visibility, Complete Control"
- "FlowLens: Lens into Your DevOps"

### Domain Suggestions:
- flowlens.io ⭐ (recommended)
- flowlens.com
- flowlens.dev
- getflowlens.com

## Alternative Names (Ranked)

### 2. **CoreFlow**
- **Pros:** Professional, suggests core platform, easy to brand
- **Cons:** Less distinctive, "Core" is overused
- **Tagline:** "CoreFlow: The Core of Your DevOps"

### 3. **UnifyFlow**
- **Pros:** Clear value prop (unification), emphasizes flow
- **Cons:** Longer, less catchy
- **Tagline:** "UnifyFlow: Unify Your DevOps Stack"

### 4. **FlowStack**
- **Pros:** Modern, suggests full stack, tech-forward
- **Cons:** "Stack" is generic
- **Tagline:** "FlowStack: Your Complete DevOps Stack"

### 5. **DevFlow Platform**
- **Pros:** Descriptive, professional
- **Cons:** Longer, less brandable
- **Tagline:** "DevFlow: Where Development Meets Operations"

**Key Messages:**
